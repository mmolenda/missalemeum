About the project

Website Missale Meus has been established for God's greater glory and for people's avail. It attempts to put together complete liturgical calendar as well as all propers and other texts used in the Tridentine Mass in one place and to present them in a comprehensible manner.

English and Latin texts come from venerable website Divinum Officium. Some parts of Ordo have been borrowed from Fish Eaters. Project's codebase is available in github repository. This website has been built as a private project and it does not represent any official organization or group.
